Donations

By donating, you will help us acquire better software and equipment. Our current objective is to acquire a Sure SM-7B microphone. For every £10 you donate, you will receive two additional recordings in addition to your initial complimentary recording. If you donate £5, you will receive one extra recording. This offer is valid up to £20 donation. Beyond that, for every £20 you donate, you will receive three extra recordings on top of that.

We believe in providing a service that you can trust, which is why we are offering a complimentary recording. All you have to do is send us your script or text via email and we will return the recording to you for free within 1-24 hours or 2-7 business days.
